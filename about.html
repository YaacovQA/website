Short presentation of me :

Allow me to introduce myself briefly. My name is Yaacov Mettoudi, and I am currently 28 years old. I reside in Hadera, and I am happily married with two adorable daughters. My eldest is 3 years old, while my youngest is just 7 months. At the age of 18, I had the privilege of serving in the IDF's Golani Brigade, which is part of the Israeli Defense Force.

So, here are some things I really enjoy doing:

  1. Traveling and exploring different cultures all over the world. It's amazing to experience new places and learn about their unique traditions.
  2. Nature is like my personal zen zone. I find so much peace and tranquility when I'm surrounded by the beauty of the outdoors.
  3. Cooking is one of my absolute favorite activities, especially when I'm whipping up something tasty with my wife. It's so much fun and brings us closer together.
  4. Music is like therapy to me, even though I'm no pro. I just love playing instruments and losing myself in the melodies.
  5. I'm totally hooked on mangas! I can't get enough of those Japanese comics. They're a gateway to their fascinating culture.
  6. Oh, and martial arts! I'm into both practicing and watching. There's something so captivating about the discipline and physicality of it all.
